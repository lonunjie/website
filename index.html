Protect life. Seed it.

What is the purpose of life?

We can look close and care for our families. It helps preserve our species.Or look at the big picture. So far we are the only space rock discovered to host life, and an abundance of it. Yet human activities accelerated the extinction of species rather than preserve it. Protect the diversity of life.Think about the future. Will we be in a position to seed life across the universe? This will require scientific discoveries and technological advances. We will need to cooperate, to build a future for life to bloom.It’s a big task. Let’s do what we can. Let’s plan for the future of life.
